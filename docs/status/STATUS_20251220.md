## API entry points
- **Core API** (default base `/api/core`, direct port 8001): health at `/health` and `/api/core/health`; DB and Celery checks at `/health/db` and `/health/celery`; Celery enqueue probe `/api/v1/health/enqueue`; Prometheus metrics at `/metrics` (alias `/metric`).
- **Auth-host** (default base `/api/auth`, direct port 8002): health at `/api/auth/health` and `/api/v1/auth/health`; legacy `/health` alias; OpenAPI at `/api/auth/openapi.json`.
- **AI service** (default base `/api/ai`, direct port 8003): health endpoints `/api/v1/health`, `/api/v1/live`, `/api/v1/ready` plus aliases at `/api/ai/health` and `/health`; metrics at `/metrics`; scoring routes under `/api/v1/score` (proxied via gateway `/api/ai/api/v1/...`).
- **Gateway (nginx)**: exposes `/health`, `/metrics` (static `gateway_up 1`), proxies `/api/core/`→core-api, `/api/auth/`→auth-host, `/api/ai/`→ai-service, legacy `/api/v1/`→core-api, and serves SPAs under `/admin/` and `/client/`.
