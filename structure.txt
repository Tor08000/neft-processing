NEFT Processing repository structure (short form)
================================================
- docker-compose.yml (local stack: gateway, auth-host, core-api, ai-service, workers, db, redis, minio, observability)
- platform/
  - auth-host/ (FastAPI auth service)
  - processing-core/ (core-api service, migrations, business logic)
  - ai-services/risk-scorer/ (risk scoring stub)
  - billing-clearing/ (Celery workers/beat shared image)
- gateway/ (nginx reverse proxy)
- services/
  - flower/ (monitoring UI)
- shared/python/ (shared pydantic models + helpers)
- infra/ (otel, prometheus, grafana config)
- frontends/
  - admin-ui/
  - client-portal/
- docs/ (design and operational notes)
- scripts/ and *.cmd helpers (developer tooling, curl runners)
- db/ (local Postgres volume mountpoint)
